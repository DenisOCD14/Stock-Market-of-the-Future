Economy

The forces moving markets.

Zoom out from individual stocks and you see the tectonic plates — automation, demographics, energy, and policy — that decide where the whole market drifts over decades.

Big-picture drivers

Six forces to watch this decade

Automation & AI

Machines augmenting knowledge work could lift productivity — and reshape which jobs and companies thrive.

Demographics

Aging populations in rich nations and youthful growth elsewhere are redrawing the global economic map.

The energy transition

The shift to electrified, renewable power is one of the largest capital reallocations in history.

Central banks & rates

Interest-rate decisions ripple into every asset price — the gravity that pulls on the whole market.

Deglobalization

Supply chains are being rewired for resilience over pure efficiency, with lasting price effects.

Digital money

Central-bank digital currencies and stablecoins could reshape payments, savings, and monetary policy itself.

How to read it

Macro is a compass, not a clock.

Big trends tell you the likely direction of travel — they're hopeless at timing. Use them to stay oriented, not to jump in and out of the market.

  • Trends unfold over years; markets react in minutes
  • Consensus is often already priced in by the time you read it
  • Diversification is how you respect a future no one can predict
  • The goal is to stay invested through cycles, not to dodge them
